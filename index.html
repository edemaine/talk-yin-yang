Complexity of Puzzles

  • $>30$ pencil & paper puzzles proved NP-hard
  • Many relate to other problems in TCS:
    • Numberlink [Kotsuma & Takenaga 2010] —
      vertex disjoint paths
    • Spiral Galaxies [DLS 2015], Tatamibari [ABDDLL 2020] — partition into rectangles
    • Slitherlink [Yato & Seta 2003], The Witness [ABDHHKLR'18] — Hamiltonian path
    • Masyu [Friedman 2002] — angle-restricted tours
    • Path Puzzles [BDDHKK 2020] — discrete tomography

Related Graph Partitioning

  • Often looking for partitions which are connected, balanced, and compact
  • NP-complete to find balanced partition in grid graphs for:
    • 2 colors in weighted graphs, even with only three rows [BLLS 1998]
    • 3 colors in unweighted graphs [BNP 2018]
    • $k$ colors in solid grid graphs [Feldmann 2013]
  • Polynomial-time algorithms for approximating balanced partitions in planar graphs [HZGX 2021]

  • Reduce from TRVB in 4-regular planar multigraphs, which is NP-complete
  • Given a graph, is there a set of vertices that can be broken so the resulting graph is a tree?
